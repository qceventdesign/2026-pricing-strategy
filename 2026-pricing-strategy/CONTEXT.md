## Decision Rules (Summary)

- **Add visible management fee:** Programs >$40K with onsite staffing/multi-day/design work. Range: $2,500–$10,000. Fortune 500 clients expect it.
- **Absorb into markup:** Programs <$40K or returning clients where a new line item creates friction.
- **Walk away / minimum fee:** True Net Profit negative after OpEx. Minimum program fee: $1,500–$2,500.
- **Flex below floor:** Only for strategic relationships with recurring high-value work. Document the decision.

## P&L Formula

```
Client Invoice Total
− Client Commission (if any)
− GDP Fee (6.5%)
− Total Vendor Costs
= QC GROSS REVENUE → QC Margin %
− Estimated OpEx (hours × $90)
= TRUE NET PROFIT → True Net Margin %
```
